
Hey Guys, 

Here the last update before our meeting tomorrow at 9am Zurich time.

Why Robhack?
To have fun! 
But also to gain vision for the future submission to the Future Emerging technologies (FET) grant proposal -- a EU-FET program with deadline May 13 2020. Robhoot's dream is to be trained to be a open-source technology to help solving complex problems for humanity. Yeah!

I have committed a Robhack guideline file (see below). The file outlines a procedure for complementing steps between the pragmatic and the Robhoot scenarios. As a consequence of all the discussions during the Robhack's preparation, the proposal is moving towards "Causal Learning in Federated Networks" or something like that. What is all this about? 

Imagine having ALL datasets and ALL causal models to respond YOUR question. Would it be possible in the rapidly evolving digital ecosystem? Imagine each of us running a node with YOUR own solution. Can we learn from maaanny maaaany nodes with the same general interest than each of us? Which is the architecture generating the most robust knowledge about YOUR question? Which are the incentives to proceed this way in comparison with the existing way of generating our own research?


The procedure is more complete for the first two days -- Architecture and Data Integration. The other days are now in a very early stage. Therefore, the updates for the other modules, Bayesian Space Models and Visualziation and Reporting will come after the discussion during the first two days.
